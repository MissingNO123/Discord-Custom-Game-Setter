To get your token:
Open Discord in your web browser, click on the address bar and clear it, then type
javascript:alert(localStorage.token) A message will pop up with your token in quotation marks.
It will look something like this:
"MTc1DeF2GHIjKLm3NOP4QRS5.Tuv6WX.y7Z_ABcDE7-fgHij8K8LMNopQrs"
Copy it and paste it here without the quotation marks.
